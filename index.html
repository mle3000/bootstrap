About Me

My name is Emily. I love cooking. I also have two dogs. Whenever I am in the kitchen preparing a meal you will find one or both of my dogs waiting for the perfect moment to grab a quick snack. Whether it's an intentional drop or an accidental slip those dogs are ready to pounce. While there are some foods that should NEVER be given to dogs, there are lots of great snacks we can share with our four legged friends. Here we will showcase some great recipes and ingredients everyone in the family can enjoy.

Meet our Dogs:


Kilroy

Kilroy is an American Hairless Terrier. His favorite food is ground beef. His favorite pasttime is chasing squirrels. He doesn't understand why he has never been able to catch one.

Velvet

Velvet is also an American Hairless Terrier. His favorite food is watermelon. He also likes to eat leafy greens like kale. Velvet is shy but don't let him know you can give pets or he will bug you all day.
